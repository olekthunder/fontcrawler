What is fontcrawler?

FontCrawler is a web application allows you to fetch fonts from other sites


How to use it?

log in and come to home page. Form for requests should appear.
Then, enter page's link you want to parse and press "Get Fonts" button.
Now, go to your profile to "User Fonts" tab and do whatever you want with those fonts :)
